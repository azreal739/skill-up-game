Mentor voice

Mission briefings, questions, hints, feedback and Help Centre entries read aloud — each mentor with their own voice, generated by a small AI voice model (Kokoro‑82M) running entirely on this computer. Nothing you do is sent anywhere: the model is fetched once (bundled in the offline package; a one-time download on the web), and every generated line is cached on this device so replays are instant. Look for the small speaker buttons next to text throughout the Academy — they show a spinner while a line is being generated and let you pause/resume playback.
